About Me

I help startups and small businesses build professional websites and applications at affordable prices.

My focus is on quality, transparency, and long-term support for every client.

My Mission

I come from a small city in Bihar, where many local businesses still struggle to access modern technology.

I have personally seen shop owners, startups, and entrepreneurs wanting websites and apps but being unable to afford high development costs.

That experience shaped my mission.

My goal is to make professional digital solutions affordable and accessible for small businesses and startups worldwide.

I offer flexible pricing and installment-based payments so founders can grow first and pay comfortably later.

I believe technology should empower businesses, not burden them with heavy costs.

When my clients grow, I grow with them.
